Uninstalling Programs

Often, there will be unwanted programs and applications on the images. Remember: never uninstall a program that is mentioned as required in the README. You also should not uninstall: VMware tools [INCLUDE: the other shit].

Also get rid of the %APPDATA% files

Control Panel

The best place to uninstall unwanted programs is in Control Panel. To navigate to the Programs and Features page while in categories view, click on "Uninstall a program" below "Programs". To navigate there from icons view, click on "Programs and Features".

From here, double-click on the app you want to uninstall, then click [YES] when prompted. Follow the instructions on any uninstaller that opens, making sure to remove as many associated files as possible.
